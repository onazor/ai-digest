**🎙️ Kani-TTS-2: 400M Param Open-Source TTS with Voice Cloning in Just 3GB VRAM**  
nineninesix.ai has released Kani-TTS-2, a 400M-parameter open-source text-to-speech model that delivers high-fidelity speech while running in as little as 3GB of VRAM. The model treats audio as a language and includes voice cloning support, offering a lightweight alternative to traditional compute-heavy TTS systems.

**🤖 Self-Hosted OpenClaw Assistant: Secure WhatsApp-Based AI on Your Own Hardware**  
OpenClaw is a self-hosted personal AI assistant that runs on users’ own devices and integrates with messaging platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, Slack, and Discord. It can answer questions, automate tasks, and interact with local files and services while keeping data under user control instead of in third-party clouds.
